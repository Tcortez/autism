What is Autism?

Autism is a complex developmental disability that is the result of a neurological disorder that affects the brain's functioning. It typically appears during the first two years of life and impacts development in the areas of social interaction and communication skills.

There are five Autism Spectrum Disorders:
  1. Autism
  2. Asperger Syndrome
  3. Pervasive Developmental Disorder Not Otherwise Specifed (PDD-NOS)
  4. Rett's Disorder
  5. Childhood Disintegrative Disorder

About Us

We are a non-profit group that helps families in the Las Vegas area who's lives are affected by Autism. We hold monthly meetings at the Main library branch of Clark County in Las Vegas, NV. We are here to help, so feel free to join us. Our organization is always looking for volunteers and donations. Remember, every little bit helps.

We also hold a yearly 5k Run/Walk to raise money for our local chapter. There are games, food, and live music for everyone to enjoy. Donations are welcome but not mandatory to attend this event. It is more of a way for us to meet and greet the community and raise awareness about our cause.
